Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 23, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes what data we collect, how we use and protect your information when using the OrbitVPN service.

We take your privacy seriously and make every effort to ensure the security of your data.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Telegram ID: Unique identifier of your Telegram account for authorization
  • Telegram Username: For identification and customer support
  • Payment History: Transaction information for financial reporting
  • Subscription Data: Active plan, expiration date, subscription status
  • VPN Configurations: Technical data about created configurations

3. Data We DO NOT Collect

We respect your privacy and DO NOT collect:

  • Logs of your online activity
  • History of visited websites
  • IP addresses when using VPN
  • Session times and traffic volume
  • Content of transmitted data
  • DNS queries
No-logs policy: We do not maintain or store logs of your online activity.

4. Use of Data

Collected data is used for:

  • Providing and managing the VPN service
  • Processing payments and financial accounting
  • Providing technical support to users
  • Improving service quality
  • Sending important service notifications

5. Data Storage and Protection

All data is stored on secure servers using modern encryption methods. We apply the following security measures:

  • Data encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Database encryption
  • Regular backups
  • Server access control
  • Regular security audits

6. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We DO NOT sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Payment Systems: For processing payments (TON, Telegram Stars)
  • Legal Requirements: When there is a court order

In such cases, we only share the minimum necessary information to perform the specific task.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our service operates through a Telegram bot and does not use cookies or other tracking technologies in web browsers. This web page is used for informational purposes only.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Data Access: Request information about stored data
  • Data Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all data
  • Data Export: Obtain a copy of your data

To exercise your rights, contact our support service through the Telegram bot.

9. Referral Program

When using the referral program, we store information about who invited the user and award bonuses according to the program terms. Referral relationships are confidential and not disclosed to third parties.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy. We will notify users about significant changes through the Telegram bot. The last update date is indicated at the beginning of this document.

11. Contact Information

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the processing of your data, please contact us through:

  • Telegram Bot: Command /support or "Support" button in the menu
  • Support Channel: @your_support

12. Consent

By using our VPN service, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any of the terms, please do not use our service.
